================================================================================
ANCLAJE DE ESTILO (PEGAR AL INICIO DE CADA PROMPT)
================================================================================

Clean modern cartoon vector style for a mobile game UI: bold defined outlines
(2-4 px equivalent), flat color fills with soft cel-shaded highlights and a
single drop shadow underneath, saturated yet friendly palette, slightly chunky
and rounded shapes, hand-drawn but crisp digital finish. Centered composition,
transparent background, no text, no watermark, no signature. Match the look of
premium casual mobile gacha games like Cookie Run: Kingdom or Brawl Stars icons
but slightly softer.
